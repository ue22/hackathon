About Us

At theend.page, we understand that every goodbye carries a different emotion : joy, sadness, anger, anxiety, or calm. That’s why we’ve created a space where you can craft a personal farewell page that matches how you truly feel. To bring your emotion to life, we’ve introduced five expressive chibi characters each representing one core feeling. When you select a chibi, the color theme of the website shifts to reflect that emotion, creating an atmosphere that feels just right for your message. Whether you’re writing a peaceful goodbye, a fiery rant, or a bittersweet note, theend.page lets you leave with color, creativity, and meaning.
